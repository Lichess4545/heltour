About

Lichess leagues are a place for players to organize long chess games on lichess.org.

The leagues are managed by volunteer moderators. We use Slack as our main form of communication.

We are officially a part of the lichess.org site ("Lichess").

Visit the league home pages for more information about each league.

Lichess4545.com is an open source project. Find us on Github.

The site is built using Django. Icons from icons8.
