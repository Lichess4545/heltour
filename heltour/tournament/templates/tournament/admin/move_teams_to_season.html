What happens on move:

  • The team's season is reassigned to the target season (across leagues if applicable).
  • Team members, board order, captain/vice-captain flags, contact info, and seed rating are preserved.
  • Players are auto-registered (SeasonPlayer) in the target season if not already.
  • Slack channel is cleared (was tied to the source season).
  • Team number is reassigned to the next available number in the target season.
  • If the team name collides in the target season, a counter is appended (e.g., "Team Name (2)").
  • Team invite codes follow the team — their league/season are rewritten to the target.

Move is blocked when the team has any of:

  • Team pairings (as white or black)
  • Team byes
  • Alternate assignments or alternate searches
  • Knockout seedings or advancements
  • Multi-match progress records
  • A non-zero TeamScore (any tiebreak/points/match count)
